How it works
Simple process.
Real results.
01

You onboard us

Tell us who your ideal client is, what you charge, and what makes you different. We learn your agency inside out before we make a single call.

02

We build your prospect list

We research and compile a list of businesses that match your ideal client profile — the right size, right industry, right budget.

03

We call on your behalf

Our team makes outbound calls, pitches your agency, handles objections, and qualifies leads — all representing your brand professionally.

04

You get the lead or booking

When a prospect is qualified and interested, we hand them directly to you — warm, briefed, and ready to talk. You close the deal.

05

You pay on results

Flat fee per qualified lead delivered, plus a percentage of the first month's contract value when they sign. No results, no full payment.

What a qualified lead looks like

Decision-maker confirmed

We only pass leads where we've spoken to the actual owner or decision-maker — not a receptionist or middle manager.

What a qualified lead looks like

Interest confirmed

They've expressed genuine interest in your service, not just agreed to "maybe" hear more. They know the price range and still want to proceed.

What a qualified lead looks like

Call booked or warm handoff

Either a discovery call is booked directly in your calendar, or we do a live warm transfer to your team right then and there.

Example: You run a social media agency. We call 200 local businesses in your city, pitch your $1,500/month package, qualify 12 interested prospects, and book 12 discovery calls directly into your calendar. You pay a flat fee per call booked, plus 10% of the first month's value for each one that signs.
